Responsiveness

Layouts can be made repsonsive by declaring additional classes of the form col-n-x where n is in [xs, sm, md, lg] and x is a number between 1 and 12.

Example

A arrangement of cells that is 4 across on large and medium screens, 2 across on small screens and 1 across on xs screens.
Notes:

  • We don't need to declare a col-lg-x class, since we don't desire a different behavior than we have for medium size.
  • We also do not need to explicitly declare col-xs-12. This is the default behavior on extrasmall screens.
  • This example also illustrates row nesting.
